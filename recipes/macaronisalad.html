macaroni salad

description

macaroni salad is a beloved dish cherished for its simplicity and nostalgic charm. it embodies the essence of summer picnics and backyard barbecues with its refreshing combination of tender macaroni pasta, crisp vegetables, and tangy dressing. the pasta, typically elbow macaroni, is cooked to al dente perfection and then tossed with a medley of colorful ingredients such as crunchy celery, sweet bell peppers, and pungent red onions. the dressing, often a creamy blend of mayonnaise, mustard, and vinegar, coats each ingredient, imparting a creamy richness and a delightful tanginess to every bite. whether served as a side dish or a standalone meal, macaroni salad delights the palate with its vibrant flavors and satisfying textures, making it a perennial favorite for gatherings and potlucks alike.

ingredients

  • uncooked elbow macaroni
  • mayonnaise
  • white sugar
  • white vinegar
  • distilled white vinegar
  • prepared yellow mustard
  • salt
  • ground black pepper
  • chopped celery
  • chopped onion
  • seeded and chopped bell pepper
  • grated carrot
  • chopped pimento peppers

steps

  1. gather all ingredients.
  2. bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. rinse under cold water and drain.
  3. mix mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, salt, pepper, and macaroni together in a large bowl.
  4. stir in celery, onion, green pepper, carrot, and pimentos.
  5. refrigerate salad for at least 4 hours before serving, but preferably overnight.
